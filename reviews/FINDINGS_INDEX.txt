  Severity:   CRITICAL

              source strategy). No audit trail. Confirms 30K target.
  Fix Time:   1 hour
  Status:     ❌ Not Fixed (New Issue)

[SEC-3] RLS Policies Use 'USING (true)' - Authorization Bypass
  Severity:   CRITICAL
  Files:      supabase/schema.sql:363-382
              supabase/schema-v3.sql:50,58
  Issue:      All RLS policies grant "authenticated USING (true)" — any logged-in 
              user can read/write ALL data (creators, shipments, referrals, etc.)
  Impact:     Privilege escalation. Junior team member can modify creator tiers,
              commission rates, payouts. Defeats entire RLS model.
  Fix Time:   3 hours
  Status:     ❌ Not Fixed (New Issue)
